Houston Sights

Houston Sights is an Android app that connects citizens and visitors to a better experience of all Houston has to offer by generating customized, voice-narrated tours based on user preferences and location. Built during the 2016 Houston Hackathon.

safeHOU

safeHOU is an android app that uses the Google Maps API, public crime data, and machine learning to predict the likelihood of crime occurring in Houston based on location and time-of-day. Created during the 2017 Houston Hackathon.

World Generator

This is a small java program that utilizes OpenSimplexNoise to generate random maps of tropical islands with a simple humidity map for biomes.
